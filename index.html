Fall for Main St.

Join us on October 23rd for Fall for Main St!

There will be a puppy adoption center, pumpkins and gourds for sale, kids face painting/arts and crafts, and multiple local artisans.

Featured Farm: Strawberry Hill USA

Joining us at Fall for Main Street will be Strawberry Hill USA. Strawberry Hill U.S.A. is the largest strawberry farm in SC with 115 acres. Peaches are still their primary crop with close to 1,000 acres in production. They have also ventured into growing summer squash, cucumbers, cantaloupes, beans, and PUMPKINS!

Market on Main

     Market on Main will be hosting Fall for Main Street. Market on Main Spartanburg provides a boutique space for artisans to sell their curated and hand-crafted goods. Owner Lisa Giaimo integrates her Midwest roots with Spartanburg's southern charm to bring an ever-changing and unique shopping experience to downtown. With the expertise of store stylist and owner, Stacey Johnson, Market on Main creates a welcoming environment that features curators with a wide variety of items for home décor, clothing, art, gourmet foods and gifts. 

Involving the Community 

Volunteers for Fall for Main St. include many students from the Scholars Academy, such as Harrison Scott, Amelia Gay, Elein Padilla, and Emma Potter. Strengthening the downtown community is pertinent to Market on Main, so allowing students to gain real-world experiences while having a great time is wonderful outlet for positive impact.
